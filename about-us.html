About Us ICAN

Inspire Communications and Awareness Network (ICAN) is a social enterprise whose mission is to pioneer dissemination of specialized knowledge, employing strategic platforms for informed awareness, with the aim to extend valuable assets of know-how and expertise to inspire sustainable methods and choices essential to the well-being of people, environment and enterprise.

ICAN is founded on the power of one word – inspiration. And the recognition that inspiration is at the heart of everything great. Our name reflects our purpose – to inspire people, organizations and communities to make better choices for social, environmental and economic well-being.

We also realize that merely disseminating information about development plans would not result in development. Oftentimes, the conventional method of information, education and communication for development programs is itself insufficient to achieve the desired results. In addition, people need motivation to accept development. 'Development Motivation' and 'Development Awareness' are essential aspects of development communication. Hence Inspiration and Awareness creation are the core elements of our social development mission.

Our Social Themes

Eco-Living

Promotion of sustainable management of environment and natural resources, and to inspire responsible lifestyle choices.

Personal Development

Nurturing essential life leadership principles that inspire people to access their best potential in all facets of life.

Economic Empowerment

Building essential capacity for effective enterprise development, sustainable economic gain, and improved livelihoods.


INSPIRE COMMUNICATION AND AWARENESS NETWORK
