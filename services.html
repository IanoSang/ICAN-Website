Our INITIATIVES

Career Guidance and Advisory Program

This is a performance focused career guidance and advisory program for aspiring professionals. Focused on the demands and objectives of employment, our programming currently consists of performance-oriented training and development, coaching, professional as well as entrepreneurial mentorship.

Life Skills Program

Life Skills Series comprise of comprehensive character focused approaches that concentrate on nurturing the skills and principles essential for performance and success in personal as well as professional life.

Life Skills: Foster-to-Independence Transition (FIT)

With special regard to youth who are ‘aging out‘ of the (foster) care systems, often finding themselves instantly on their own with little or no sustenance from family or community, our specialized Life Skills training serves the vital need for adequate transition preparation to enable these youth cope with the demands and challenges of ‘independent living’

INSPIRE COMMUNICATION AND AWARENESS NETWORK

ICAN designs and delivers programs that prepare and connect young people with a broad range of career opportunities.
